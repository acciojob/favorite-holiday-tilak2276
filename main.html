Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is renowned for its romantic atmosphere, rich history, and iconic landmarks. Known as the "City of Light," Paris offers a perfect blend of art, fashion, culture, and cuisine that captivates visitors from around the world.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – the most iconic symbol of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – home to the famous Mona Lisa painting.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – a vibrant avenue for shopping and dining.
  5. Seine River Cruise – an enchanting way to see the city’s beauty.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – buttery and perfectly flaky pastries.
  • Baguette – a classic French bread staple.
  • Macarons – colorful and deliciously sweet treats.
  • Crêpes – thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • French Onion Soup – rich and comforting with melted cheese.
